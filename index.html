About the Show

The Distortion Cellar is a weekly internet radio show.
It's a place to hear some fun tracks and discover some gems.

The main genres are Garage Rock, Punk, and Soul, but no musical genre is off limits.
There will be themed shows that lock into a genre so people can enjoy their favs.
Some shows will be a Mixed Bag of loosely connected songs.
