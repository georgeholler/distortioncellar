Recurring Segments

The regular features you can count on episode after episode.

  • Last Call

    A final song for the listener to take out of the cellar.
    The song will usually be longer and more reflective.

  • Mondegreen Alert

    A Mondegreen is a name for a misheard lyric
    Wikipedia - Mondegreen
    Mondegreens are usually funny and sometimes appear to be improvements
    One example is in the Jimi Hendrix Experience's Purple Haze when he says "Excuse me while I kiss the sky". Many people heard, "Excuse me while I kiss this guy".

  • Strange Brew

    An odd song.

  • Cellar Echoes

    A song played by multiple artists
    Sometimes an original and then a cover.
    Sometimes several versions in different genres.

  • Cellar Discovery

    A newly released song, or something new to the listener.

  • Reread the Label

    Sometimes you've heard a song a million times and then one day you hear something you never heard before, or someone tells you something you missed.
    These songs will be examined to find alternate meanings, or researched stories that give more context by the songwriter.

  • Dusty Bottle

    An old song that is a classic or may be unknown to the listener
