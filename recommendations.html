Recommendations

Records, artists, and more worth checking out.

  • Music Streaming

    I have used Spotify to listen to music for over 10 years and recommend it.

  • Radio

    I have listened to Little Steven's Underground Garage (LSUG) on Sirius/XM channel 21 since 2015.

    What distinguishes this station is:

    • The variety of music
    • All the DJs are highly knowledge. Either musicians, famous DJs, or musical scholars
    • They take the time to give info about the songs, who wrote it, when it was released, who produced it, and so much more. They also tell stories that are highly entertaining.

  • LSUG DJ Shows

    Honestly, every DJ and their shows are amazing.
    Do yourself a favor and try them out.

  • Internet Radio Station

    Sonic Fringe on Luxuria.com
    The show is curated and hosted by Moana Santana.
    It is a live show from 6-8pm (Eastern) on Tuesdays and Moana spins a fresh, highly eclectic variety of artists and tracks.
    You cannot listen to her show without going "Wow" many times.
