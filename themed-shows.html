Themed Shows

Special episodes built around a single idea, era, or genre.

  • Mixed Bad

    Not really a themed show, but loosely connected songs.
    Some initial shows will be like this as I learn the DJ ropes.

  • Years

    Songs from a specific year.
    The shows will focus on the significance of the songs in that year and how they changed the musical landscape.
    Some shows will focus on a genre and some will be multi-genre.

  • Decade Time Machine

    Songs from a specific decade.
    The shows will take the listener on a journey through time to enjoy music.
    Some shows will focus on a genre and some will be multi-genre.

  • Songwriters

    Shows focused on a songwriter or songwriter team

  • Covers

    Shows dedicated to the covers of a certain artist

  • Events/Venues

    Shows focused on special events, like festivals or famous venues

  • Genre Deep Dive

    A deep dive into a genre that the listener can enjoy familiar songs, or learn about the genre.
    Some planned example genres are:

    • Blues
    • British Invasion
    • Classic Rock
    • Funk
    • Garage Rock
    • Glam Rock
    • Goth/Darkwave
    • Jazz
    • New Wave
    • Outlaw Country/Country
    • Punk
    • Prog Rock
    • Rap/Hip Hop
    • Reggae
    • Rockabilly
    • Shoegaze
    • Surf Guitar
    • Soul
    • Power Pop
