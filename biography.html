Mini Biography

George is a lifetime music enthusiast.
He was a child in the 70s, and a teenager in the 80s.
He has soaked up music and genres since childhood.
He has listened to Little Steven's Underground Garage on Sirius/XM since 2015 and has learned a wealth of musical knowledge about many genres.

Although sadly he doesn't play any musical instruments, he decided to bring music to people by creating the Distortion Cellar.
Just don't ask him how he created a cellar in south Florida.
